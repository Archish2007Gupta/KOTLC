Dig into the New York Times bestselling series that's taking the world by storm!

The Keeper of the Lost Cities series is all about friendships and finding the strength within yourself. Not to mention perseverance, determination, and endless adventure. So, what are you waiting for? Let's get started!

Gather like-minded fantasy lovers for an epic adventure! The books transport readers to a vibrant world where elves live in Lost Cities like Atlantis and others forgotten by or unknown to the human world. Our main character, Sophie Foster, slowly discovers the secrets of her past as well as the potential perils of her future, and gains a band of friends along the way who have just as many secrets as she does.

Sophie Foster, who lives in San Diego with her family, just doesn't fit in. She's a lonely twelve year-old prodigy with a photographic memory and a secret ability to read minds. So she's thrilled to learn of a place where she might belong: a vibrant world of elves who live in Lost Cities like Atlantis. But moving there isn't easy. Even in the Lost Cities, Sophie stands out among her new friends because of her mysterious upbringing and amazing telepathic talents.
